Crockpot Italian Chicken

description

super simple three ingredient recipe for those busy nights where you want something delicious without putting in too much effort.

ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle italian dressing (such as Olive Garden Italian Dressing
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

steps

  1. stir italian dressing and parmesan cheese together in bowl
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken. Cover and cook on Low until chicken is tender, no longer pink in the center, and the juices run clear, about 8 hours.
